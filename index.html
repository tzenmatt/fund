Candestra Ltd. is a privately held parent company founded and led by Dimitrios Paolo. Our objective is to compound capital at an attractive rate of return by deploying it into our highest-conviction opportunities, through equity stakes or asset-specific investment vehicles. We follow a value-oriented philosophy, seeking complexity that is mispriced or misunderstood. Where conviction is high and inefficiency is clear, we size significantly. Because true opportunities are rare, we operate with a long-term time horizon and a willingness to be inactive when nothing meets our standards. Prospective investors must be aligned with this approach; otherwise, Candestra is not the right fit.
