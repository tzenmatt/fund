Candestra Capital is a privately held investment firm founded and led by Dimitrios Paolo. We operate under Candestra Ltd., with the objective of compounding capital at an attractive rate of return by deploying proprietary capital into high-conviction opportunities through equity stakes or asset-specific vehicles.

We follow a value-oriented philosophy, seeking complexity that is mispriced or misunderstood. Where conviction is high and inefficiency is clear, we size significantly. Because true opportunities are rare, we operate with a long-term time horizon and a willingness to remain inactive when nothing meets our standards.

Candestra Capital currently invests proprietary capital. Future partners must be deeply aligned with this approach.
